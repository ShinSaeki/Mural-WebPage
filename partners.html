Mural Inc.

We, Mural Inc., aim to transcend every wall in the world. Now, looking around, there are many walls in the world. It is not only a building wall, but a language wall, a culture wall, a race wall, a border, etc. Also, walls have been created in various concepts and phenomena such as cities and nature, life and death, good and evil, and so on. But a rich culture is what comes when you overcome these barriers. I think that the feeling that people and goods that were shared try to connect again is the entrance to a rich world. The form in which the thought is expressed in the most straightforward and natural way is not a primitive mural. It was the first art to which it was entrusted the desire to connect with the unknown world, the outside world. As we paint murals, we want to overcome the various walls of modern society. The mission is to present the way of new culture towards the world.
